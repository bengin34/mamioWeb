Frequently asked questions

How do I track left and right side?

Tap the left or right button on the home screen to start and stop a feeding session. The app remembers which side is next.

Where do I see the last feeding time?

The home screen always shows how long it has been since the last session and which side was used.

Can I add notes to a session?

Yes. After a session ends, you can add a short note. Notes are stored locally on your device.

How do I export a report?

Go to the Stats or History screen and use the export option to generate a report you can share with a doctor or consultant.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. Mamio works fully offline. All data is stored on your device and no internet connection is required.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally. Clear it by going to your device settings and clearing the app's data, or uninstall the app.

What languages does the app support?

Mamio supports 14 languages: English, Deutsch, Español, Français, हिन्दी, Italiano, Português, Русский, Türkçe, Українська, العربية, עברית, 日本語, and 한국어.

Delete data

All data is stored locally on your device. To erase it, clear the app data in your device settings or uninstall the app. We have no copy of your data.
