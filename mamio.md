Mamio is a simple, offline baby feeding and baby care tracker made for real life.

We know caring for a baby can be beautiful, exhausting, emotional, and messy all at once. Mamio was designed with mothers and parents in mind: for sleepy nights, busy days, tired hands, and the small details you do not want to keep in your head.

What started as a focused breastfeeding tracker has grown into a calm daily companion for your baby's routine: feeding, bottles, diapers, solids, sleep, pumping, growth, health records, vaccines, doctor visits, and reminders.

Designed for real nights, not perfect days.

Mamio is built for 3:00 AM feeds, one-handed logging, and moments when you have zero mental energy.
No complicated setup. No learning curve. Just start, stop, or quickly log what happened, then get back to your baby.

Diaper Photo Tracking
When something looks off, capture it. You can now add a photo to any concerning diaper change — a small thumbnail appears in your diaper history so you can spot it at a glance. Tap the photo to see it full-size, and swipe through your photo history when you need to show a doctor a week of changes in one visit. Photos are saved privately on your device with location data removed.

Diaper Color Guide
Not sure what you're looking at? A new color guide inside the diaper entry screen explains what yellow, tan, green, red, black, and white stool may mean — with clear normal, monitor, and urgent labels so you know when to call your doctor and when to relax.

Tummy Time Tracker
Build your baby's strength one small session at a time. Mamio now helps you track supervised tummy time with a live timer, position choices (floor, chest, carry, or lap), daily progress, and AAP-recommended age-based goals (15-60 minutes per day). Enable the optional module in Settings when your baby is ready.

Tummy Time stats and insights
The Stats page now turns tummy time into a clearer routine: see total sessions, total minutes, average per day, goal days, daily progress bars, age-based goal lines, positions used, and practical insights to help you stay consistent.

Mother and Baby stats
The Stats page is now easier to scan with separate Mother and Baby views. Keep nursing and pumping patterns focused in one place, while baby care stats like bottles, diapers, sleep, and Tummy Time have their own clearer space.
